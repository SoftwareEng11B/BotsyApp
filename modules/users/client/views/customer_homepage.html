Botsy Mural Making Process

When requesting a mural, there will be a three step process. This process will be coordinated by an administrator that will guide you along the way.

Once you sign in and upload information, you begin the mural request by either uploading an image for the mural, making your design for the mural or requesting a graphic designer to make one for you.

Once the wall design is finalized, a Botsy owner will then trace the outline of your mural onto the wall. The Botsy robot is connected to the wall and will outline the whole mural in pencil.

Lastly, a talented painter will come in and color in the mural as desired at a time that is best for you. Then, your office, room or whatever space will be bright with new life!
