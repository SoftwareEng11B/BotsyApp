Steps To change YOUR GAME

When requesting for a mural, there will be a three step process. This process will be coordinate by an administrator that will guide you along the way

Once you sign in and upload information. You begin the mural request by either uploading an image for the mural, making your design for the mural or requesting for a graphic designer to make one for you.

Once the wall design is finalized. Then a Botsy owner will then trace the outline of the mural onto the wall.

Lastly, a talented painter will come in and color in the mural as desired. Then, your office, room or whatever space will be bright with new life!
