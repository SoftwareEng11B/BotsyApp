Botsy Mural Making Process

When requesting for a mural, there will be a three step process. This process will be coordinate by an administrator that will guide you along the way

1. Customers will be able to have the choice to get a graphic design make a design for thier mural by submitting information on what they want.

2. Once the wall design is finalized. Then a Botsy owner will then trace the outline of the mural onto the wall.

3. Lastly, a talented painter will come in and color in the mural as desired. Then, your office, room or whatever space will be bright with new life!
